MEET
JESS

I didn't build Jess's Mobile Bar just to bartend.

I built it because I believe gathering people around good drinks, good energy, and good storytelling is sacred work.

With years of experience in the hospitality industry, I've worked every shift, worn every hat, and learned every lesson. I created Jess's Mobile Bar because I saw a gap: people wanted elevated bar experiences without the pretension, and they deserved a vendor who was as invested in the vibe as in the logistics.

Whether it's a wedding, a corporate event, or a celebration, I bring the kind of hospitality that makes people feel seen. From the cocktails to the connections—everything I do is intentional.

I built this business with people in mind—my clients and every guest who walks up to the bar.

The trust you place in me is never taken lightly. Whether it's a one-night celebration or ongoing service, I show up fully, pour with purpose, and lead with genuine care.

I'm so happy you're here. Let's pour into something unforgettable.
